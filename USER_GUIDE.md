Welcome! This guide explains how to use our WhatsApp bot to book, manage, and cancel your doctor appointments easily.

---

## üöÄ Getting Started

1.  **Save our Number**: Add our WhatsApp number to your contacts.
2.  **Say Hello**: Send any message (e.g., "Hi", "Hello", "Book appointment") to start.
3.  **Main Menu**: The bot will reply with a menu. Tap **"Book Appointment"** to begin.

---

## üìÖ How to Book an Appointment

Booking is simple and takes less than a minute!

### Step 1: Choose a Specialization
- The bot will ask what type of doctor you need.
- Select from options like **Cardiologist**, **Dermatologist**, **General Physician**, etc.

### Step 2: Select a Doctor
- You'll see a list of available doctors for that specialization.
- Select the doctor you wish to see.

### Step 3: Choose a Date
- The bot will ask for a date. You can:
    - Type a date like **"15th August"** or **"next Monday"**.
    - Or simply **select "Tomorrow"** or other quick options if provided.

### Step 4: Pick a Time Slot
- The bot will show **available time slots** for that day (e.g., 10:00 AM, 2:30 PM).
- Tap the time you want.
- *Note: If a time is not shown, it is already booked or unavailable.*

### ‚úÖ Confirmation
- You will receive a **Booking Confirmation** message with:
    - Doctor's Name
    - Date & Time
    - Appointment ID (keep this safe!)
- You will also receive a Google Calendar invite if you use Google Calendar.

---

## ‚ùå How to Cancel an Appointment

1.  Send a message like **"Cancel appointment"** or select **"Cancel"** from the main menu.
2.  The bot will show your **upcoming appointments**.
3.  **Tap the appointment** you want to cancel.
4.  You will receive a confirmation message that it has been cancelled.

---

## üîÑ How to Reschedule

To change your appointment time:
1.  **Cancel** your current appointment (see above).
2.  **Book a new appointment** with the new date/time.
